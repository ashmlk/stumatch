Data Deletion

If you want to remove your account or some of your data, you can manage them here. Please note that JoinCampus does not hold onto any user data after deletion and actions on this page are permanent and irreversible.

Permanently delete all your posts

Deleting your posts is permanent. By deleting your posts are related data (likes and comments) will also be removed. Once you delete your posts you cannot retrieve them back and they will be permanently removed.

Permanently delete all your post comments

Deleting your comments is permanent but it will not affect your posts. However, all the replies will be removed and they cannot be retrieved again.

Permanently delete all your post likes

This will remove your likes from all the posts. This action is permanent and you cannot reverse it.

Permanently delete all your blogs

Deleting your blogs is permanent. By deleting your posts are related data (likes and replies) will also be removed. Once you delete your blogs you cannot retrieve them back and they will be permanently removed.

Permanently delete all your blog replies

Deleting your replies is permanent but it will not affect your blogs. However, all the replies will be removed and they cannot be retrieved again.

Permanently delete all your blog likes

This will remove your likes from all the blogs. This action is permanent and you cannot reverse it.

Permanently delete all your courses

This will remove you as in enrolled person in all the courses

Permanently delete all your courses reviews

This will remove your replies on all the courses. This is permanent and your replies will be deleted from our database.

Permanently delete your account

Deleting your account is permanent. By following the further steps all your data will be removed form our database. You will not be able to retrieve any information after you delete your account. When deleting your account, all content associated with your account will be removed permanently. Please note this is permanent and we cannot reverse this action.
