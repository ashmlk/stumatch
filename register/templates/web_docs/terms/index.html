Terms of Service

Welcome to JoinCampus!

JoinCampus builds a platform for students to connect, find new friendships, and grow their network throughout their university experience. These terms govern how our users can use our platform and the services it offers. Unless stated otherwise that separate terms apply (and our terms do not), the benefits are provided to you by JoinCampus.

We do not charge our users for the services we offer; the website is entirely free. We do not sell any data to third-party businesses, and we do not show them any data that can uniquely identify you.

Our Data Policy explains how we collect, store and use our users data across our platform.

Services that we provide

Our mission and goal are to help students connect and expand their social circle and network. To help advance this mission and reach this goal, we offer specific services to you, which are detailed below:

  • Connect you with students that matter to you: We help you connect with students with standard courses with or are in the same university as you. We use the information that you provide us with upon registration. We do not use any other data and do not collect data on your activity across our platform.
  • Help you express yourself and talk about what matters to you: There are several ways on JoinCampus to express yourself and talk about what matters to you. Our users can share posts, with 4 images. Users can also write blogs about subjects they enjoy and can share it with their friends.
  • Discover interesting content: We have pages that show you exciting content based on specific criteria. You will be able to find content that is liked by most of our users, and they find them interesting.
  • Combat harmful content and protect our users: Our main goal is to provide a safe platform for users to express themselves and build networks. People will join our forum if they feel safe. We take harm, bullying, and sharing inappropriate content very seriously, and if we learn content and conduct like this, we will take action. For instance, we may remove the content, restrict access, and in extreme cases, we will remove a user from our platform. We take this very seriously and try our best to provide a safe community for our users.
