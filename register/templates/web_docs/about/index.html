Who are we?

A platform built for students. We are continually iterating and creating new ways to connect students. Our goal is to make it easier for students to discover different opportunities to expand their networks and plan their future.

What we stand for. What we believe and work to accomplish.

Our Mission

We strive to bring creativity, discoverability, and support to students. We try to make our platform more accessible every day and create a safe environment for students. We want student voices to be heard and create unique experiences during their time at university.

Our Values

At JoinCampus, we highly value trust, integrity, and passion. We work to provide a platform built upon trust, which provides services and support for everyone. We highly respect and value authenticity, and we want all students to be proud of who they are and what they achieve.

Our Goals

JoinCampus's primary goal is to create a a safe platform for all students to create content, share their ideas on courses and find support in all areas. We want to ensure students that they always will have a safe platform to express themselves, find friends, and expand their network.
