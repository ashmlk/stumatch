Account Privacy

By setting your account to Public all users will be able to see your posts except users you have blocked. Your content will be shown to other users on the Top, Hot and University pages as well.

By setting your account to Private your posts will only be shown to your friends and will not be shown to the public. Users can only see your name in their search results and your content will not be shown to them. We will not use your content in our Top, Hot and University pages.
