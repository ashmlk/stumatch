Data Policy

This policy describes the type of data we collect and the information we process to support JoinCampus.

Sharing
Will we share your data with colleges or universities (Designated Learning Institutions)?

We will not or are obligated to share your data with your DLI. Our only focus is to support students and their privacy, and we will make our best effort to provide users with the safest experience.

  • My Information and interactions: We will not inform any institution about your interactions and connections on our platform, and our main focus is to deliver maximum privacy for our users.
What do we collect?
Things thats you provide.

We do not collect any information on our users, other than data gathered on registration. We do not also ask users for any data. In case our policy changes, registered users will be notified via their registered email address.

  • Connections: We do not collect any information's about you on the pages you visit, content and people you interact with and your communications across our platform.
  • Provided Data: We only use your University and Program name that you provided upon registration in order to connect you to other users across our platform.
  • Usage: We do not collect any information about you on the pages you visit, content, and people you interact with and your communications.
  • Devices: We do not collect information on devices you use to interact with our platform. We do not gather information on data such as your device operating system, hardware and software versions, signal strengths, and browser type. However, based on your device specifications, some features integrated into our platform may not work correctly.

    We do not collect any unique identification about your devices and will not ask you to provide them.
  • Partners: JoinCampus is not affiliated with any third-party applications and does not send or sell others your data.
How we use the data you provided?

The data that we use is the only information that you provide during registration. We only use the data from those fields to connect students as much as possible and create an engaging platform. We do not collect any data from your computer or use your browser information. The data we use are solely provided by you, at your discretion. You may edit or remove those data at all times.

  • University Information: We use your university only to connect you with other students who are in the same university as yours.
  • Program: We use your program name to find students in your program and share courses with them.
How do we store your data?

Your data is safely stored in our database with an appropriate encryption level to ensure maximum safety for our users. All the information you provide during registration and interaction are safely stored in our database.

  • Safety: Delivering maximum privacy and safety is extremely important to use. We gather and store all crucial information, such as your password, in an encrypted format. This information cannot be access by anyone.
  • Account Deletion: Yes. You can delete your account permanently from our servers. Keep in my that this is Permanent and is not reversible. Once you delete your account, all your data will be deleted from our database.
  • Content Deletion: Yes. You can delete the content you posted all at once through our settings. Please note that this action is permanent and is not reversible. Once you delete any content that you posted, all the related data will be deleted permanently from our database.
